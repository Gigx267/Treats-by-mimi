Our Mission

At Treats by Mimi, we believe that life's sweetest moments deserve a touch of magic. We're not just bakers; we're architects of joy, crafting delectable treats that spark smiles, fuel laughter, and forge lasting memories.

To celebrate life's milestones, big and small: From birthday candles flickering on frosted masterpieces to the shared joy of a warm croissant on a Sunday morning, we're here to make every occasion a little sweeter.

To nurture a community of kindness: Our bakery is more than just a counter and a display case; it's a haven for shared laughter, warm connections, and the simple pleasure of sharing a treat with loved ones.

To inspire through creativity: We're constantly pushing boundaries, experimenting with flavors and textures, to bring you innovative delights that awaken your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

To bake with a conscience: We believe in using the finest, locally sourced ingredients, treated with respect and sustainability in mind. Every bite is not just delicious, it's a reflection of our commitment to the earth and our community.

Treats by Mimi isn't just a bakery; it's a promise to sweeten your life, one delicious bite at a time. We invite you to step into our haven, explore our world of handcrafted delights, and let us be a part of your sweetest moments.
